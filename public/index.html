WHY THIS?

I wanted a simple, fast way to find the best tool for a startup task on demand and So I created StartupToolchain.


WHY IS MY FAVOURITE TOOL NOT HERE?

This list is opiniated towards getting a product to the market without much technical debt by a indie, solopreneur and bootstrapping startups. This is not an exhaustive list of startup tools, but best tools for a startup task based upon above criteria. If you think your favourite tool belongs to this list then please tweet to me.


WHY IS THIS CATEGORY NOT LISTED HERE?

This toolchain will never be completed, But I will keep adding tools until I can. The category you're looking for might get added then.
Please open a discussion on GitHub on new category addition.


BIAS

I try my best to limit my personal bias in the selection of the tools, but like every other human activity I cannot eliminate bias completely. So, I'm happy to receive positive criticisms to help fix the biases. I often find new tools, categories through recommendations from the users who have subject matter experience.
